How to make Pizza

Description

Pizza are increasingly popular among college students. From chocolate chip cookies to macadamia nut cookies, there is an enormous variety of cookies you can make. It does not require a lot of time either!

Also make sure to have tamarind sauce to mix it all in together. The resulting dish will taste as delicious as it looks!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oven
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare pizza dough by washing and letting it sit in water for 30 minutes
  2. Cut shrimp
  3. Crush peanuts
  4. Heat up wok for 5 minutes
  5. Throw in flat noodles and stirfry for 3 minutes
  6. Throw everything else in
  7. Place beansprouts and crushed peanuts on top after its plated
