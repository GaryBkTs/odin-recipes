How to make Chocolate-Chip Cookies

Description

Cookies have always been a popular treat anywhere you go. From chocolate chip cookies to macadamia nut cookies, there is an enormous variety of cookies you can make. It does not require a lot of time either!

Also make sure to have tamarind sauce to mix it all in together. The resulting dish will taste as delicious as it looks!

Ingredients

  • Cookie dough
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oven
  • Mixer
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Prepare dough by washing and letting it sit in water for 30 minutes
  2. Cut chocolate chips
  3. Crush sugar
  4. Pre-heat oven for 5 minutes
  5. Throw in flat noodles and stirfry for 3 minutes
  6. Throw everything else in
  7. Place beansprouts and crushed peanuts on top after its plated
